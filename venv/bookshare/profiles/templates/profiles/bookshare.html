Welcome To

Book Share

One man’s trash is another man’s treasure.

Make a difference : Keep the cycle spinning

BookShare is an innovative way to help customers lower their education and entertainment cost by buying and selling used books. Customers can buy or sell books through the website’s e-classified system.

Be it academic or recreational, journals or novels, we all have lots of books stuffed in our bookcase and don’t have any idea what to do with it. Most of the books that were used in our school or colleges become useless to many of us and we treat it as a trash.

This website aims to solve the gap between buyer and seller by making a bridge between them where a buyer and seller can directly contact each other for the books.

What's new

There are many of writers in Nepal and huge amount of books are published each year. There are thousands of book enthusiasts around Nepal, but they are unaware about all the available books and novels as many people might not have time to visit library frequently. So, we strive to bring out the solution: digital advertisement of books and novels. This will connect readers with publishers in a single platform such that no user should waste there time searching for the books they want.

A new feature called mystory helps people to share there story. So if you think you are a good writer or you have a great story to share with us and everyone please click Mystory section in the top right corner where you can share your story with us and if the story is worth sharing it will be featured in our website.
